Welcome to Math Review!

This website is designed to help 11th grade students strengthen their knowledge in Algebra, Geometry, and Probability. You'll find learning materials, practice exercises, and helpful videos that make math easier and more fun.

We would like to give special thanks to Mr.Dai, our dedicated math teacher. He teaches Calculus and has been an important supporter of this website. With his guidance and encouragement, this platform has grown into a useful resource for students.

Math Review is a learning platform created for 11th grade students. It focuses on strengthening knowledge in Algebra, Geometry, and Probability, while also providing guidance for advanced topics like Calculus. Our goal is to make math easier, more engaging, and to help students build confidence through videos, examples, and practice resources.
